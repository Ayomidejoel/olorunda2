Description

Olorunda Local Governnment is one of the best local government in osogbo in Osun state. Olorunda Local Governnment is made up of districts, town and villages which includes Igbona which is the administrative headquartes and Oba-ife, Oba-Oke, Abegunde, Ajala, Ajegunle, Aliu, Aminu, Amusan, Aro, Elemo, Idiape, Jagun, Odesola, Odofin, Okemole, Osolo, Saba.

Olorunda Local Governnment Area has its administrative seat located in Igbona, which is at the outskirts of Osogbo, the capital city, and is made up of the incumbent Executive Chairman, the Councillors and other honorable memebers that work together for the betterment, development and progress of the communities under its jurisdiction.
The postal code of the area is 230
